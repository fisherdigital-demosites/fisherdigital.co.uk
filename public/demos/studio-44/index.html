Our Story

A salon born from Stokes Croft

Studio 44 opened in 2021 with a simple idea: a salon that feels like your mate's living room, but with better scissors. We're not a chain. We don't do corporate. We do good hair in a space that reflects the creative energy of the neighbourhood.

Our team of four stylists bring a mix of editorial training and street-level creativity. Whether you want a classic bob, a skin fade, or something you found on Pinterest at 2 AM — we're into it.

Walk-ins are always welcome when we have space. For colour or specific times, book ahead via WhatsApp or call.
